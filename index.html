Grading Reference

Severity Levels

Negligible

Can only be seen with magnification.

Tiny

Can barely be seen but is obvious with magnification.

Minor

Obvious but very minor and does not significantly detract from the appeal.

Moderate

Obvious and detracts from the presentation/appeal of the book.

Extensive

Damage too bad to be considered moderate but not bad enough to be major.

Major

Unsightly and significantly impacts the presentation/appeal/readability.
